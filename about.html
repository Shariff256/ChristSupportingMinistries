About Christ Supporting Ministry

Christ Supporting Ministry is a Christian-founded organization based in Kabarole and Kyenjojo Districts, carrying out activities such as healthcare support and preaching the Gospel of God.

The organization began as a special calling from God revealed to Mrs. Kyakyo Beatrice. After sharing with the church, it was confirmed that supporting orphans, vulnerable children, women, and youth was part of God’s mission.

Achievements of Christ Supporting Ministry

The organization is fully registered from LC1 to the National level.

It has a bank account in Barclays Bank and a strong network with organizations such as UTOPIA, FACES Africa, World Vision, KIND Uganda, RICNET, Baylor Uganda, Stride for a Family Health, and TDI.

Christ Supporting Ministry acquired an office at Mbuzi Trading Centre.

In 2012, the organization received vocational training tools from England to support a training school in Kabarole District.

It also received medical equipment donations from a medical center in England.

The ministry has empowered youth in Mbuzi and Mugoma through income-generating skills such as poultry, goat rearing, and piggery.

A credit and savings scheme was established to help women in Kabarole access small loans for business.

The ministry also started a church—**Christ Church of Hope International**—in Nyantugo, Kyenjojo District, with support from Bishop Worthington in England.

Challenges Faced by Christ Supporting Ministry

  • Limited financial resources to support ministry projects.
  • Insufficient transport means for field outreach.
  • Few training materials for vocational programs.
  • Lack of enough medical supplies for health support activities.
  • High number of vulnerable community members requiring assistance.
  • Limited long-term sponsorship for ongoing projects.
